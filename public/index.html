How to use this app

1

Choose a study mode

Use Flashcards for individual recall, Family ↔ Genus to learn taxonomy groups, or Binomial ↔ Common to connect scientific and everyday names.

2

Use the flashcards

Choose the question direction and optionally filter by family. Tap the card to reveal the answer. Press Got it, Again, or Previous.

3

Turn speech on

Select an installed voice, test it, then switch speech on. The app reads the front of each card and reads the answer when the card flips.

4

Play the matching games

Tap one item in the left column, then its match in the right column. Correct pairs stay completed. Change the round size or press New round whenever you like.

5

Use quick update commands

On the main menu, type simple commands such as “turn speech on”, “open the family game”, or “add Corymbia ficifolia | Red-flowering Gum | Myrtaceae”. Tap 🎙️ and allow microphone access when Android asks. Speak naturally and the command is applied when you stop talking.

6

Edit or import the list

Open Edit / Import List to add trees manually, replace the list, remove entries, export a backup, or restore the built-in 80-tree list.

7

Your progress is saved

Correct answers, attempts, best streak, voice choice and edited tree data are stored in this browser on this device.
